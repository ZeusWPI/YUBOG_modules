# On the Subject of PlateUp

In This Module a Customer Comes in and Orders a Dish.
You Don't See the Dish Though.

## Figuring Out the Dish
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| <ins>**First Customer**</ins><br/>If there is a parallel port and there is a vowel in the serial number, then the dish is <ins>a rare steak</ins><br/>Otherwise if the bomb has more than 6 batteries, then the dish is <ins>a medium steak</ins><br/>Otherwise if there are 3 numbers in the serial number, then the dish is <ins>a well done steak</ins><br/>Otherwise the dish is <ins>a pizza</ins>               |
| <ins>**Second Customer**</ins><br/>If the first customer ordered a steak and there is a DVI-D port, then the dish is <ins>a pizza</ins><br/>Otherwise if the products of all digits in the serial number mod 6 is bigger than 3,        then the dish is <ins>a medium steak</ins><br/>Otherwise if there is a PS/2 Port, then the dish is <ins>a well done steak</ins><br/>Otherwise the dish is <ins>a burger</ins> |
| <ins>**Third Customer**</ins><br/>If the first character of the serial number is in the first half of the alphabet and the last digit of the serial mod 3 is 1, then the dish is <ins>a burger</ins><br/>Otherwise if it's autumn and there is an even amount of ports, then the dish is <ins>a medium steak</ins><br/>Otherwise the dish is <ins>a pizza</ins>                                                       |

## Recipes

### Burger

 - Take the Meat
 - Put the Burger on the Hob
- Put the Burger in a Bun
- Plate It and Serve

### Steak

 - Take the Meat
 - Select the Hob
 - Let It Cook on the Hob for 1 to 3 Times Depending on the Type of Steak.
   - You will know when it's done, when the process color becomes orange.
- Plate It and Serve

### Pizza

 - Take Some Flour
 - Knead (Chop) the Flour
 - Add Oil
 - Take Tomatoes
 - Chop Twice to Make Sauce
 - Take Cheese
 - Chop Cheese
 - Select the Hob
 - Plate It and Serve
