On the Subject of Plateup!

  • In This Module a Customer Comes in and Orders a Dish
  • You Don't See the Dish Though

Figuring Out the Dish

  • If the First Letter of the Serial Code is in the Last Half of the Alphabet and the Last Digit of the Serial Key Mod 3 is 1 the Dish is a Burger
  • Otherwise if There Are 2 or More Battery Groups and at Least 2 Groups Have 2 Batteries, the Dish is a Pizza
  • Otherwise if There Are Exactly 3 Ports, the Dish is a Rare Steak
  • Otherwise if There is a Parallel Port the Dish is a Medium Steak
  • Otherwise the Dish is a Well Done Steak

    • Recipes

    Burger

  • Take the Meat
  • Put the Burger on the Hob
  • Put the Burger in a Bun
  • Plate It and Serve

    • Steak

  • Take the Meat
  • Select the Hob
  • Let It Cook on the Hob for 1 to 3 Times Depending on the Type of Steak.

    • You will know when it's done, when the process color becomes orange.

  • Plate It and Serve

    • Pizza

  • Take Some Flour
  • Knead (Chop) the Flour
  • Add Oil
  • Take Tomatoes
  • Chop Twice to Make Sauce
  • Take Cheese
  • Chop Cheese
  • Select the Hob
  • Plate It and Serve
