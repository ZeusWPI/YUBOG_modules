# 4D Bomb radar
<f>Under attack by a fourth-dimensional nation? Accidentally started an interdimensional war?
We might not be able to stop it, but we can help you avoid getting hit!
Follow this quick guide to learn how to detect and dodge incoming 4D bombs.</f>

The radar shows a 3×3 grid representing your immediate surroundings.
The following table shows which squares around the icon will be hit. (X means DEATH !!!).<br/>
So if the upper middle square has an X the square above the symbol on the radar will be hit!

Your mission as a bomb defuser is to determine which tiles are safe and click on one that won’t be struck by any bomb.
