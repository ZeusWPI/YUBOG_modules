4D Bomb radar

Under attack by a fourth-dimensional nation? Accidentally started an interdimensional war? We might not be able to stop it, but we can help you avoid getting hit! Follow this quick guide to learn how to detect and dodge incoming 4D bombs.

The radar shows a 3×3 grid representing your immediate surroundings. In the table below, each row shows a bomb type: the left column displays its symbol, and the right column shows which positions around it will be hit (X means DEATH !!!).

Your mission as a bomb defuser is to determine which tiles are safe and click on one that won’t be struck by any bomb.
